WHAT WE DO

We will design and build a one-seater solar car that's fully dependent on solar energy. This car will be designed to race on
the Texas Motor Speedway over the course of 4 days during the summer (2025 event). Throughout the season, we will
research, design, and manufacture a race-capable solar car for the event in Dallas.

WHY WE NEED YOUR HELP

We need YOUR help to support us financially as we compete in the High School Solar Car Challenge in Texas! Building
and racing a solar-powered car requires funding for materials, tools, travel, and competition fees. Your donation will enable
us to bring this project to life, allowing students to gain hands-on STEM experience while advancing sustainable technology
and awareness. Together, we can drive innovation and inspire future problem-solvers!
